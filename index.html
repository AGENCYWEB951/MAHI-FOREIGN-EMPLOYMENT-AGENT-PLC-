Value Proposition

MAHI PLC acts as a dependable bridge between eager, qualified Ethiopian workers and premier recruitment agencies across Saudi Arabia, Lebanon, and Kuwait. We manage the entire recruitment pipeline with rigorous attention to technical qualification, legal clarity, and human safety.

The B2B Partner Portal & Pipeline

Gulf recruitment offices require legally cleared, thoroughly competent workers. We execute a strict, compliant onboarding pipeline completely integrated with state regulations.

01

Agency Partnership

Verify the Gulf agency/sponsor through the Ethiopian Embassy. Generate and upload the Job Order onto the official Ethiopian E-LMIS platform.

02

Sourcing & E-LMIS

Source candidates legally. Ensure strict adherence to the minimum age threshold (25 years old for Gulf domestic workforce profiles).

03

Medical & TVET

Candidates pass official GAMCA medical fitness exams and complete the mandatory 21-day training via certified TVET networks to secure CoC badges.

04

Visa & Deployment

Process credentials via Tasheer / Enjaz / MoFA systems. Complete final air tracking logistics exclusively via Ethiopian Airlines.

Eligibility Requirements Checklist

  • **Age Requirement:** Minimum 25 years old for Gulf domestic roles.
  • **Passport Status:** Valid, unexpired Ethiopian Passport.
  • **Education:** Completion of 8th-grade basic education standard.
  • **Medical Records:** Clean background check and fitness approvals.
Zero Recruitment Fees Charged to Workers In complete compliance with Ethiopian Law, we never charge recruitment fees, flight costs, or medical processing bills to domestic workers. All overhead is covered by the employer.
